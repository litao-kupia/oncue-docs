# Welcome to OnCue

OnCue is an **invisible advisor** for live conversations on Windows. It listens to your
call, transcribes both sides in real time, and quietly suggests what to say next — on a
transparent overlay that **stays hidden from screen sharing and recording**.

Use it for sales calls, interviews, support, negotiations, or any high-stakes conversation
where the right line at the right moment matters.

## What it does

* **Live transcription** — your microphone and the other side's audio, captured and
  transcribed as you talk.
* **Real-time advice** — short coaching cues and a drafted reply, ready the moment you
  need them.
* **Invisible overlay** — excluded from Zoom, Google Meet, Teams, OBS, and OS screen
  recording. The call only ever sees your shared window.
* **Sessions** — scoped working contexts with their own guideline, knowledge, and a
  summary + action items when you finish.
* **Channels** — pull in Slack threads alongside the live call.
