# Welcome to OnCue

OnCue is a living memory of your work relationships for Windows: it listens to your calls and channels, remembers everyone you talk to and what was said, and tells you what to say next — in your voice, the moment you need it.

Everything is captured on your machine. Nothing is auto-sent. The overlay stays off screen shares. The more you use it, the sharper it gets.

Built for sales, customer calls, interviews, 1:1s, negotiations, partnerships — anywhere the right words matter.

## What it does

* **Relationship memory** — every person becomes a dossier (role, style, temperament, what they care about), built automatically. See your network as an interactive graph.
* **Commitments** — tracks what you owe each person and they owe you, and resurfaces it before you talk again.
* **Replies in your voice** — drafted from your persona, tuned per person, and biased toward the angles you actually pick.
* **Briefs & live awareness** — walk in knowing where things stand; quiet flags during the call catch contradictions, commitments, and dodged questions.
* **Live transcription** — your mic and the other side, speaker-tagged with real names.
* **Channels** — Slack and Telegram threads feed the same memory.
* **Knowledge** — notes, files, folders, or a pasted link ground every reply.
* **Sessions** — scoped working contexts that produce a summary + action items.
