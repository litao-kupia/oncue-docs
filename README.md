# Welcome to OnCue

OnCue is a **living memory of your work relationships** for Windows. It listens to your
calls and channels, remembers **everyone you talk to** — who they are, what was said and
promised, and how you relate — and uses that memory to tell you **what to say next, in
your voice**, the moment you need it.

The more you use it, the sharper it gets. Everything is captured **on your machine**, and
nothing is ever auto-sent.

Use it for sales, customer calls, interviews, 1:1s, negotiations, partnerships — anywhere
the relationship, and the right words, matter.

## What makes it different

Most assistants forget the moment a call ends. OnCue **remembers** — and that memory is
what makes every reply better than the last.

* **Relationship memory** — every person becomes a dossier (role, style, temperament,
  what they care about, and your relationship), built automatically from your
  conversations and DMs. See your whole network as an interactive graph.
* **Commitments** — OnCue tracks what you owe each person and what they owe you, across
  calls and channels, and resurfaces it before you talk again.
* **In your voice** — replies drafted from your persona and tuned per person; it even
  learns which suggestions you actually pick.
* **Pre-call briefs & live awareness** — walk in knowing who you're meeting and where
  things stand; quiet flags during the call catch contradictions, commitments, and dodged
  questions.
* **Private by design** — capture and memory live on your device; nothing is auto-sent,
  and the overlay stays off screen shares.

## Also in the box

* **Live transcription** — your mic and the other side's audio, speaker-tagged.
* **Channels** — Slack and Telegram threads feed the same memory.
* **Knowledge** — notes, files, folders, even a link (e.g. a team page) ground every reply.
* **Sessions** — scoped working contexts with a summary + action items when you finish.
