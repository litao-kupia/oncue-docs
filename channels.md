# Connecting channels

OnCue can pull conversations from your communication channels so they flow into a session
alongside the live call.

## Slack

1. Open the desktop console and go to **Channels**.
2. Connect Slack. OnCue auto-detects workspaces you're signed into in your browser, so in
   most cases there's nothing to paste.
3. When starting a session, add a **Slack thread** as a source.

Once connected, new messages in the followed thread are fed to OnCue, and the advisor can
draft replies for them in context.

> Telegram and Discord support is planned. Slack is available today.

## What OnCue reads

Only the threads you explicitly follow for a session. OnCue does not browse your entire
workspace — it watches the specific conversation you point it at.
