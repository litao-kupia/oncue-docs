# Advisor & replies

During a conversation OnCue gives three kinds of help, each from a separate engine:

* **Advice** — real-time coaching cues: what to probe, what to address, where things are heading.
* **Replies** — drafted responses you can send or say, in your voice.
* **Live awareness** — quiet, fact-grounded flags. See [Live awareness](awareness.md).

## Replies

Press your reply hotkey (or ask on demand) to draft a reply for the current moment, grounded in:

* the live transcript,
* your session **guideline** and **knowledge**,
* the latest **advice**,
* the **dossier** of whoever you're talking to (see [Relationships](relationships.md)).

Instead of three near-identical voicings, OnCue offers a few distinct **angles** — lead, direct, details, pivot, future — each tagged with one word, so you pick the approach, not the wording.

### It learns what you send

Drafts are **editable in place** — tweak, then copy. OnCue treats what you actually send as signal:

* **Learned reply style** — records which angle, phrasing, and length you pick, and biases future drafts that way. Dashboard → **Persona → Learned reply style**.
* **Per-person style** — learns how you reply to *each* person and tunes drafts accordingly. **Persona → Per-person style**.

Local-first, tied to your account, wiped on sign-out.

## Persona & people

* **Persona** — tune OnCue's tone and formality so replies sound like you (console → **Persona**).
* **People** — OnCue adapts per person automatically; add your own handling notes under **People**. Manual notes are never overwritten.

## Knowledge

Notes, files, folders, PDFs, or a pasted link ground advice and replies. See [Sessions](sessions.md#attachments) and [Knowledge](relationships.md#knowledge-sources).
