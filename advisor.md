# Advisor & replies

During a conversation OnCue gives you three distinct kinds of help, each from a different
engine:

* **Advice** — coaching cues on how you're handling the conversation.
* **Replies** — drafted responses you can send or say, in your voice.
* **Live awareness** — quiet, fact-grounded flags when something matters. See
  [Live awareness](awareness.md).

## Advice

As the conversation unfolds, the advisor surfaces short, real-time cues — what to probe,
what to address, where the conversation is heading. These keep you oriented without reading
a wall of text.

## Replies

OnCue drafts a reply you can use directly. Replies are grounded in:

* the live transcript,
* your session **guideline** and **knowledge**,
* the latest **advice** (so the suggested reply reflects current coaching),
* the **dossier of whoever you're talking to** (see [Relationships](relationships.md)).

Instead of three near-identical voicings, OnCue offers a few distinct **angles** — lead,
direct, details, pivot, future — each tagged with a single word, so you choose the *approach*
rather than splitting hairs over wording.

Press your reply hotkey, or ask for a reply on demand, to generate one for the current
moment.

### It learns what you actually send

The draft is **editable in place** — tweak it, then copy. OnCue treats what you actually
send as its signal:

* **Learned reply style** — it records which angle and phrasing you pick, and biases future
  drafts toward what you genuinely choose. If you routinely trim drafts shorter (or expand
  them), it notices and adjusts length too. See it on the dashboard under
  **Persona → "Learned reply style."**
* **Per-person style** — it also learns how you like to reply to *each specific person* —
  which angle, what phrasing — and tunes the recommended draft when you're talking to them.
  Shown on the Persona page under **"Per-person style."**

This is local-first and tied to your account; it's wiped on sign-out.

## Persona & people

* **Persona** — tune OnCue's voice (tone, formality) so replies sound like you. Set it in
  the console under **Persona**.
* **People** — OnCue adapts per person automatically, but you can also add your own notes on
  how to handle specific contacts under **People**. Your manual notes are author-controlled
  and never overwritten.

## Knowledge

Anything you add as knowledge — notes, files, folders, PDFs, even a pasted link — is used to
ground advice and replies. See [Sessions](sessions.md#attachments) and
[Knowledge](relationships.md#knowledge-sources).
