# Custom vocabulary

Transcription sometimes mishears names, products, or jargon — a product name comes out
spelled wrong, or a person's name is garbled. **Custom vocabulary** teaches OnCue your
terms so transcripts read correctly.

## Add a mapping

1. Open the desktop console and go to **Vocabulary**.
2. Click **+ Add mapping**.
3. Enter the **heard** form (what the transcriber writes) and the **correct** term.
4. Click **Save mappings**.

Mappings apply immediately to all new transcription. Matching is **whole-word** and
**case-insensitive**, and longer phrases take priority over shorter ones.

## Refresh past transcripts

**Refresh past transcripts** re-applies your current mappings to every stored session
transcript — useful after adding a term you want corrected retroactively. OnCue reports how
many lines were updated.

## Tips

* Add one mapping per misheard spelling. If a word is misheard several ways, add each
  variant pointing to the same correct term.
* Keep terms specific — mapping a very common word can cause unwanted replacements.
