# Custom vocabulary

Transcription sometimes mishears names, products, or jargon. **Custom vocabulary** teaches OnCue your terms so transcripts read correctly.

## Add a mapping

1. Console → **Vocabulary**.
2. Click **+ Add mapping**.
3. Enter the **heard** form (what the transcriber writes) and the **correct** term.
4. **Save mappings**.

Mappings apply immediately to new transcription. Matching is **whole-word** and **case-insensitive**; longer phrases take priority.

## Refresh past transcripts

**Refresh past transcripts** re-applies current mappings to every stored transcript — useful after adding a term you want corrected retroactively. OnCue reports how many lines changed.

## Tips

* One mapping per misheard spelling; if a word is misheard several ways, add each variant pointing to the same term.
* Keep terms specific — mapping a common word can cause unwanted replacements.
