# Commitments & briefs

OnCue tracks what's **owed** between you and each person — extracted automatically from calls and channels — and puts the right context in front of you before, during, and after a conversation.

## The commitments ledger

The **Commitments** tab (with an open-count badge) lists every promise, grouped **You owe** / **Owed to you**, with checkboxes to mark done. You don't log them — they're extracted automatically.

* **Due dates & urgency** — deadlines mentioned in conversation ("by Friday") are captured; the ledger shows **overdue** and **due-soon** badges and sorts by urgency.
* **Needs attention** — opens with overdue/due-soon items you owe, plus relationships that have gone **stale while you still owe them**. Click a nudge to jump to that person.
* **One-click follow-up** — each open *you-owe* item has a **✉ follow-up** button that drafts a ready-to-send message in your voice. Review, edit, copy.

## Pre-call brief

Before a conversation: who you're meeting, what you owe each other, relationship strength, and recent context — overdue and aging commitments flagged first. Drawn from relationship memory, calendar, and recent activity.

* Meeting-starting popup → **Brief me**.
* Overlay session menu → **✦ Pre-call brief**.

## Daily brief

A start-of-day digest — what needs attention, who you're dealing with, your next meeting — appears once a day on launch, or any time from the overlay session menu as **☀ Daily brief**.

## Post-call follow-up

When a session ends, OnCue drafts a ready-to-send **follow-up** — recap, next steps, captured commitments — in your voice. In the session detail, **Copy follow-up**.
